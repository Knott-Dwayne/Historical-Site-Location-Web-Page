Leshan Giant Buddha

Location Site History

The Leshan Giant Buddha is the tallest stone buddha statue. Hai Tong's disicples scuplt the buddha out of the side of a mountain toward the east of Leshan in Sichuan Province. Before the buddha was built, locals lived around the three rives surrounding the mountain. The rivers were known to flood and people have died from the violent waves. After the budda was constructed, There is said that the waters has been calm, and safe ever since. The buddha resembles Maitreya, a famous buddha during the Tang dynasty. Maitreya is believed to bring light and happiness into people lives in the near future. This is why the Leshan Buddha was Built, to protect and watch over the locals. There has been occurrences were the buddha eyes have closed and wept during disaters; however, the buddha has smiled before and seen with a glowing halo over it's head.
